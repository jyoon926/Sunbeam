Strayed About

An egg drops from its nest to the ground, and Desmond, a young naive boy, picks it up thinking that it is just a pretty rock. He decides to keep it as a pet and brings it home. But, when the chick hatches, sad and scared knowing that her mother is nowhere in sight, Desmond realises that he must help lead the strayed chick back home.
